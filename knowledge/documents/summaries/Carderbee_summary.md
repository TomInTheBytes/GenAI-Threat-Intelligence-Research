
Report 1

Summary:
The threat actor known as Carderbee, identified in 2023, conducted a supply chain attack using the legitimate Cobra DocGuard software to deploy the Korplug backdoor on victim computers. The attackers utilized malware signed with a valid Microsoft certificate, targeting victims primarily in Hong Kong and other regions of Asia. The use of Korplug, a tool associated with multiple APT groups, adds to the novelty of this actor's capabilities. The motivation behind Carderbee's activities is information theft and espionage, with China being identified as the country of origin. The victims targeted were from various sectors in Hong Kong and Asia. The threat actor's use of sophisticated tools like Cobra DocGuard and PlugX highlights their advanced tactics. The report does not mention specific dates or operation time windows.
